MGM Muthu Hotels

MGM Muthu Hotels is a collection of luxury resorts located in some of the most beautiful destinations in the world. MGM Dizzee World

Nestled along the picturesque East Coast Road at Muthukadu, MGM Dizzee World welcomes you to a magical time in Chennai. Our Italian-designed amusement park was established in 1993 and stretches over 60 acres, captivating over a million visitors each year. Our park's allure lies in its adrenaline-pumping rides like the celebrated Rolling Thunder and Rainbow Coaster, all adhering to top-tier international safety norms. Beyond thrilling rides, our world-class Amphitheatre and unique attractions promise an unforgettable experience. Our amusement park in Chennai is a world of adventure and endless fun for every age!
