At Magnum Global, we pride ourselves on being a leading force in the maritime industry, headquartered in the vibrant hub of Singapore. Established with a commitment to excellence, integrity, and innovation, we specialize in providing comprehensive Ship Broking Services, Chartering & Brokering of Vessels & Cargoes, Wholesale, and commission-based transactions. Please note that our operations exclude participation in online marketplaces.

With a dedicated team of seasoned professionals, Magnum Global is driven by a passion for delivering tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of our clients in the shipping and logistics sectors. Our expertise encompasses a wide spectrum, from facilitating vessel charters to managing cargo logistics, ensuring seamless transactions and optimized operations for all parties involved.

As commission agents, we act as trusted intermediaries, connecting buyers and sellers while safeguarding the interests of both. Our transparent and ethical approach has earned us a reputation for reliability and excellence in the competitive world of maritime commerce.

Beyond our core services, Magnum Global extends its reach into management consulting, offering strategic guidance to enhance efficiency, mitigate risks, and capitalize on emerging opportunities within the maritime domain. We leverage our industry insights and global network to provide clients with informed perspectives that drive informed decision-making.

At Magnum Global, we envision a future where maritime transactions are conducted with unparalleled efficiency, transparency, and trust. As we navigate the seas of commerce, we invite you to partner with us for a journey characterized by professionalism, reliability, and a commitment to exceeding expectations.
